  • Presentation of Credentials

    HE Tanveer Akhtar Khaskheli (pictured), High Commissioner of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, HE Byron René Escobedo-Menéndez, Ambassador of the Republic of Guatemala, and HE Mahmoud Mohamed Aboud, Ambassador of the Union of Comoros, presented their credentials to President Tony Tan Keng Yam at the Istana on 24 April 2014.

  • Courtesy Call by Burkinabe President

    President Tony Tan Keng Yam received a courtesy call from His Excellency Blaise Compaoré, President of Burkina Faso, at the Istana on 23 April 2014. President Compaoré was in Singapore to attend the Burkina Faso-Singapore Business Forum organised by the Singapore Business Federation on 24 April 2014. He was accompanied by a delegation comprising ministers and senior officials.

  • Courtesy Call by President of French Senate

    His Excellency Jean-Pierre Bel, Senator and President of the Senate of the French Republic called on President Tony Tan Keng Yam on 22 April 2014. HE Jean-Pierre Bel and delegation members were in Singapore on an official visit from 20 to 23 April 2014 at the invitation of Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong.

  • State Visit by HM Sultan Haji Hassanal Of Barunei Darussalam

    His Majesty Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei Darussalam was on his third State Visit to Singapore from 21 to 23 April 2014. In this photo, HM Sultan Haji Hassanal accompanied by President Tony Tan, receives the ceremonial welcome at the Istana.
